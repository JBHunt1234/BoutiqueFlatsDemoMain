Frequently Asked Questions

  • What amenities do you provide?

    Our goal is to provide a turnkey suite with everything you need to run your cosmetology business in a clean, modern space.

    This includes:

    • A private lockable room in a secure facility within the welcoming shopping center
    • 24/7 site access, so you can set your own schedule
    • Stylist mirror, lighting package & cabinets
    • Retail & showcase shelving
    • A high quality shampoo bowl and chair
    • Comfortable high-end styling chair
    • Convenient dryers & hooks
    • Free laundry in an onsite & fully stocked complimentary laundry room
    • Utilities are included. Water, Electricity, HVAC
    • Free high speed Wi-Fi for you and your clients
    • Complimentary Coffee and Tea Bar
    • Break Area
  • Is laundry really free?

    Yes! We have a laundry room that our tenants use free of charge including detergent, dryer sheets, etc.

  • What size suites do you offer?

    Our suites come in a number of different sizes. Each one is unique but our smallest suite starts at about 100 sqft. Our largest is around 300 sqft. We like to describe our suites based on the number of chairs they can service. They range from 1 or 2 chairs.

  • How much does it cost to lease a suite?

    Please fill out the request info form to schedule a tour and get more details on pricing. Our suites come in a number of different sizes so pricing varies.

  • Are the suites lockable?

    Each stylist has a key code or key to their suite.

  • What are your hours of operation?

    Boutique Flats are open 24/7! Our stylists manage and operate their businesses on their own time and can bring in clients at their own choosing.

  • What requirements do you have for new tenants?

    All beauty professionals are required to have proper licensing for state and county laws, as well as business insurance and an established clientele base to support their business. In addition, all beauty professionals will be required to complete a background check.

  • What is security like?

    We have 24/7 security monitoring for the space. Clients can not enter the Flats without getting buzzed in through a callbox on the front door. Each suite is lockable.
