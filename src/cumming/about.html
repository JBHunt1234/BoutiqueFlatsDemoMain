About Us

Welcome to Boutique Flats, your premier destination for salon and beauty suites in Cumming, GA. Our mission is to provide a luxurious, professional environment where beauty and wellness experts can thrive and clients can enjoy exceptional services.

Our Story

Boutique Flats was established with a vision to create a community where independent beauty professionals can build and grow their businesses. We offer state-of-the-art suites designed to meet the needs of a variety of beauty and wellness services, including cosmetology, barbering, esthetics, and more.

Our Mission

Our mission is to support and empower beauty professionals by providing them with high-quality, fully-equipped salon suites. We aim to create a welcoming and inspiring environment for both our tenants and their clients, fostering a community of creativity, collaboration, and excellence.
